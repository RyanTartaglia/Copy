How To Copy & Paste:

Windows Copy & Paste

To copy, select text and press Ctrl and C at the same time.

To paste, press Ctrl and V at the same time.

To paste without formatting, press Ctrl, Shift and V at the same time.

macOS Copy & Paste

To copy, select text and press Command and C at the same time.

To paste, press Command and V at the same time.

To paste without formatting, press Command, Shift and V at the same time.

iOS / Android Copy & Paste

To copy, tap and hold on text, then tap Copy.

To paste, go inside a text field and click one, then tap Paste.

Linux Copy & Paste

To copy, select text and press Ctrl and C at the same time.

To paste, press Ctrl and V at the same time.

To paste in the terminal, right click and select Paste.
